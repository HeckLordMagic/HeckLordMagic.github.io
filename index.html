How COVID-19 impacted my life in both positive and negative ways
Written by HeckLordMagic on Thursday the 1st of July, 2021

COVID-19 is both good and bad. Today I will share my personal experiences on the whole pandemic.

The good

One of the best things was the lockdown. It gave me a lot of free time when I learnt to code. Online school started though only a few teachers adapted to it. We got a classroom in May. I was the leader of my schedule, something I could not say about IRL school. For a whole month I didn't go outside. Not even in my backyard.

The bad

Social distancing and masks suck. I almost choked on one. Seeing how many people we're being infected, it was very worrying. I even got COVID. It sucked that I had to stay in my room quarantined. And at that time I was moving houses.

In conclusion, COVID SUCKED. Thanks for reading I guess.
